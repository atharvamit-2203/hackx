ACME Corp — Annual Financial Report FY2025

Section 1: Executive Summary
ACME Corp reported total revenue of $12.4 billion for FY2025, representing a 14% year-over-year increase.
Net income reached $1.85 billion, with an operating margin of 18.2%.
Free cash flow was $2.1 billion, up 22% from the prior year.
The company returned $800 million to shareholders via dividends and share buybacks.

Section 2: Revenue Breakdown
- Consumer Products Division: $5.2B (42% of total revenue, +11% YoY)
- Enterprise Solutions Division: $4.8B (39% of total revenue, +18% YoY)
- Cloud Services Division: $2.4B (19% of total revenue, +21% YoY)

Section 3: Key Financial Metrics
- Earnings Per Share (EPS): $4.62 (diluted), up from $3.91 in FY2024
- Price-to-Earnings Ratio (P/E): 28.3x based on closing price of $130.75
- Return on Equity (ROE): 24.1%
- Debt-to-Equity Ratio: 0.42
- Current Ratio: 2.1
- CAGR (5-year revenue): 12.3%

Section 4: Risk Factors
1. Foreign exchange exposure — 35% of revenue is denominated in non-USD currencies.
2. Supply chain disruptions remain elevated in the semiconductor sector.
3. Regulatory risks in EU and APAC markets around data privacy legislation.
4. Rising interest rates could impact the cost of the company's $3.2B in outstanding debt.
5. Competitive pressure from emerging AI-first competitors in the enterprise segment.

Section 5: Forward Guidance
Management projects FY2026 revenue of $13.8–$14.2 billion (11–15% growth).
EPS guidance is $5.00–$5.30.
Capital expenditure planned at $1.5 billion, primarily for cloud infrastructure expansion.
The company expects to maintain its dividend payout ratio of approximately 30%.

Section 6: Balance Sheet Highlights (as of Dec 31, 2025)
- Total Assets: $28.5B
- Total Liabilities: $11.2B
- Shareholders' Equity: $17.3B
- Cash and Cash Equivalents: $4.8B
- Long-term Debt: $3.2B
- Goodwill and Intangible Assets: $6.1B
